ICOADS3.0 observations' ship tracks (1902-1911 inclusive) by month

The ship tracks of the observations located between Latitude Limits : 62 S, 30 S Longitude Limits : 70 W, 45 W in ICOADS3.0 release are shown grouped by calendar months between 1902 to 1911. The names of the ships are taken from the 'ID' field of the ICOADS dataset. For months with too many ships, ships are not labeled in the legend but instead listed as in text file located near the figure.
